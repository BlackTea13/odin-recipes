Pizza Recipe

Description

Pizza is a food with a base of dough added with tomato sauce and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 200g Pizza Dough
  • 100ml Grandma's Tomato Sauce
  • As much cheese as one can get

Steps

  1. Spread out dough into the shape of a circle
  2. Smear all the tomato sauce evenly onto dough leaving out 2cm for the crust
  3. Throw on all the cheese
  4. Bake at 230C for 15 minutes
  5. Enjoy.
