Pizza Recipe

Description

I don't know how steak can be messed up it's quite simple.

Ingredients

  • 300g Ribeye cut
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Butter/Olive oil if steak isn't too marbled

Steps

  1. Season with salt and pepper
  2. Put pan on stove and max out heat, add oil/butter
  3. Leave steak on pan for 2 minutes each side for best results
  4. Enjoy.
